Experience Italian excellence with Loro Piana’s wardrobe essentials of sleek separates, luxurious loungewear, and a whole host of cashmere classics. The brand stakes its claim on uncompromising quality, controlling every step of the manufacturing process.

Since its launch in 1924, Loro Piana has been synonymous with elegance, innovation, and the sustainable craftsmanship of the finest raw materials, including cashmere and baby cashmere, vicuña, and a variety of wools. Appealing to the sense of touch, every piece is crafted to ensure comfort and unparalleled quality.
